Who am I ?

Zubeir is naturally attracted to the technology field, his passion and commitment to the field has him stuck on researching and developing models for solving African problems using Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. His breakthrough with technology innovation happened when he developed an e-commerce platform in High school as a school project. Eversince Zubeir has been immersed with computer science and maths to sharpen his skills and experience. In 2018 Zubeir and his fellow student developed an Artificial Intelligence Healthcare solution with the likes of Ada App to help swahili speaking tech users to access health assistance through a Swahili chat bot.The innovation has been recognized by The World Youth Forum since December 2018 and had an opportunity to pitch to investors in 2019 in Egypt and Morocco in 2020. Zubeir has also got opportunities to intern in the corporate world as a corporate bank Tech assistant.
